# Pitch

## One sentence

Caribbean T0 Resilience Network compresses disaster coordination from hours of
fragmented calls into minutes of verified tasks, approvals and stakeholder
briefs.

## 60 second pitch

FutureCaribbean is about T=0: shrinking the time between signal, decision and
execution in real economies. Climate disruption is the sharpest wedge because
every hour of delay hits lives, tourism, healthcare, cargo and regional trust.

Caribbean T0 Resilience Network is the operating layer for that gap. It ingests
advisories, partner updates, inventories and requests, then creates an incident
ledger with agent-ranked tasks, owners, ETAs, evidence gaps, economic impact and
human approval gates.

The first paid wedge is hurricane logistics for hotels, ports, insurers and
emergency operators. The product starts with public and synthetic data, then
earns private integrations through paid pilots. In five years, this can become
the trusted coordination network for Caribbean resilience and a model for other
fragmented markets.
