# Pitch

## One sentence

Caribbean Coordination Desk is an agentic operating layer that helps Caribbean
operators turn climate alerts, partner updates and resource inventories into
verified tasks, decisions and situation briefs.

## 60 second pitch

The Caribbean does not lack capable people. It loses time because critical
operations are split across agencies, hotels, ports, suppliers, NGOs, calls,
WhatsApp threads and spreadsheets. During hurricanes or port disruptions, every
hour of coordination delay can affect patients, travelers, supplies and local
trust.

Caribbean Coordination Desk is the AI operations desk for that gap. It ingests
public advisories, partner updates and resource inventories, then produces a
prioritized action board with owners, ETAs, evidence gaps and stakeholder-ready
briefs. Humans approve external messages, while the system keeps the audit trail
clean.

The first wedge is hurricane logistics. The business expands into SaaS and
deployment support for hotels, ports, insurers, utilities, NGOs and emergency
operators. In five years, this can become the coordination fabric for Caribbean
resilience and a model for fragmented markets everywhere.
